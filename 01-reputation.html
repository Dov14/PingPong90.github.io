Simply put, nothing is more important to us than our reputation. Our reputation enables our firm the opportunity to serve as national and regional counsel for numerous large commercial insurance companies.

Our reputation gives us the opportunity to represent many national and local corporations and businesses to solve their problems.

Because of our reputation, Nielsen, Zehe & Antas was asked to serve as Counsel to the Mikva Commission, charged by the Cook County Board of Commissioners to lead the investigation into one of the most tragic fires in Chicago history.

We believe the single most important piece of information that we can provide to prove that our reputation is deserved is our valued and appreciated client base that turn to us to represent them and solve their problems.
